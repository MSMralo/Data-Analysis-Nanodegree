Analyzing the Stroop Effect¶

(1) What is the independent variable? What is the dependent variable?

Independant variable: the word condition congruent or congruent
Dependant variable: time taken to choose between the word conditions

(2) What is an appropriate set of hypotheses for this task? Specify your null and alternative hypotheses, and clearly define any notation used. Justify your choices.

$H_1 : p_{incongruent} > p_{congruent} $

We assume that our null hypotheses that the probability of time taken for incongruent word condition is heigher than time taken for congruent word condition.
The reason is that the person will take more time to be able to read the incongruent word condition than reading congruent word condtion, and we will run this analysis to know if we are correct about this point.
